FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Let us help answer the most common questions you might have.

I have many clients, can I purchase P12 for my clients? Will there be any additional discounts?

You can do this. We do not limit you to purchasing a P12 certificate only for yourself. We will generate a P12 certificate based on the UDID you provide, so you can generate a P12 certificate for anyone by providing the UDID of the corresponding device. When you purchase more than 10 times in total, the website will automatically provide you with a discount, so you can usually get higher resale profits.

After payment, can I apply for a refund?

Sorry, due to the special nature of the P12 certificate, we are unable to refund you once the certificate is generated. Therefore, please consider carefully before placing an order.

What is a warranty and what is it used for?

If your P12 certificate is revoked during the warranty period, we will provide you with a new P12 certificate free of charge for replacement. Note that it usually takes 3-7 days for a new certificate to be ready.

Does it work with iPhone? iPad? iPod? Mac? Apple TV?

Yes. You can buy a P12 certificate for any Apple device, it works on your iPhone, iPad, and iPod, and even you can buy a P12 certificate for your Mac and Apple TV.

Can I share this site with my friends?

You are very welcome to share AppleP12.com to your friends, through the link you share to AppleP12.com, and they successfully purchase P12 certificate, you will get a certain percentage of bonus.
